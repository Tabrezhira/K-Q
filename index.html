Frequently Ask Questions

What is Kings&Queens?

We hide the complexity to keep selling enjoyable, simple and profitable regardless of the size of your company.We are building Kings&Queens.com to become the single system that an online seller needs for his ecommerce operations irrespective of the marketplaces they are selling on.With fierce price-competition in online selling today, profits lie in running your business efficiently. We are committed to keeping our service affordable for all sizes of businesses, so you can get the edge irrespective of how big you are.

Which Marketplaces can we help you list on?

We cover the entire array of marketplaces including Amazon, Flipkart, Snapdeal, Jabong, Myntra, Amazon, Shopclues, Ebay, Rediff, PepperFry, Vilaara, Voonik, etc. We can also list your products for your own website which is running on Magento, Opencart, Shopify etc.

What is required from your (vendor’s) end?

You would need active Marketplace accounts and brand registry on these marketplaces.You would also need to share your products details in a format designed by us for your sub-category along with the images*(link or zip file).
*Images need to be as per prescribed guidelines.

How is pricing for listing services determined?

The price we quote for your project is customised to include elements like the Marketplaces you choose and the sub categories and SKUs you wish to list.

What are the payment terms?

This is a pre-paid service. The quoted amount is exclusive of taxes, government taxes as applicable will be chargeable on the invoiced amounts.

What are the basic Image Guidelines?

Basic Image Guidelines:
1. Format: jpeg
2. Dimension: 1200 *1200 or higher (300 dpi) – Higher the better
3. Clear White Background
4. Brand Name visible clearly in first image (Full product view with the product occupying almost 80% of the image canvass.)
5. No watermarks on the images
6. Different angles of the product required, especially if the product falls in the Fashion category – Full view – Side Views – Back View, etc
Caveat: Giving us e-commerce ready photography is your responsibility. If your photographs are not upto the mark, we might not take on the project. If some of your photos get rejected by the marketplace, we will not be held responsible.
